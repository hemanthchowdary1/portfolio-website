Category

Overview

SmartCart is a full-stack ecommerce platform designed to handle real-world transactional complexity, including dynamic product filtering, session-based carts, and seamless checkout experiences. Additionally, I built a responsive UI with real-time cart updates and intuitive filtering to enhance the user experience.

The E-Commerce Challenge

A major pain point in ecommerce development is managing complex cart states between anonymous session-based guest users and fully authenticated users, ensuring that a user doesn't lose their items when they finally decide to log in or register.

The Engineering Solution

I engineered a custom Django middleware utility that assigns unique tracking cookies to guest carts. I implemented a Django authentication signal that intercepts the login event, scans for guest cart tokens, and securely migrates all temporary items into the persistent database cart for that user. This ensured zero data loss and a frictionless checkout experience.
